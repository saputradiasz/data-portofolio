# Pizza Restaurant Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
Analysis of pizza restaurant sales performance, customer orders, and revenue trends to provide business insights and recommendations.

## Objectives
- Analyze total revenue and order patterns
- Identify best-selling pizzas and categories
- Understand revenue trends over time
- Provide data-driven recommendations for business improvement

## Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel**: Data processing, pivot tables, and analysis
- **Microsoft PowerPoint**: Data visualization and presentation

## Project Files
- `Pizza_Restaurant_Performance_Analysis.pptx` - Complete presentation with insights
- `project_portofolio_1_Pizza_restaurant_performance.xlsx` - Raw dataset and analysis

## Key Questions Answered
- What is the Total Revenue from sales?
- How many Total Orders were received?
- What is the average revenue per order?
- How many pizzas sold per pizza category?
- What are the top 10 best-selling pizzas?
- What is the revenue trend over time?
- What is the revenue by pizza category and size?

## Key Insights
- Classic Category pizzas are the highest contributor on the total revenue
- Large size pizzas account for nearly half of total revenue!
- Peak months: January ($71,620), March ($71,301), November ($71,005)
- Lowest month: December ($61,058) - possible seasonal decline
